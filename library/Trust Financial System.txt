Curie Trust Financial Control Center — Project Requirements Specification (PRS) v1.9.3

1) Mission

Build a local-first financial control center for the Curie Trust that continuously answers three questions with high clarity:
	1.	What is our latest net worth (right now)?
	2.	Where did our income go (by top budget category and projects/tags)?
	3.	What rate of return are we getting on our investments (before fees, after fees, after taxes)?
	4.	(Forecast) Will our capital last, and are we tax-optimized?

4) Screens (UI Requirements Summary)

4.6 Forecast (The Time Machine) - UPGRADED v1.9.3
	•	Global Settings:
		•	Birth Year, Inflation Rate, Return Rate.
		•	Tax Filing Status (Single/Joint).
		•	RMD Start Age (Default 73, configurable for SECURE 2.0 compliance).
	•	Strategy Configuration:
		•	Withdrawal Order: Standard (Taxable -> Deferred -> Roth) vs. Legacy Saver (Deferred -> Taxable -> Roth).
		•	Roth Conversion Strategy: "Fill to Bracket Top" (12%, 22%, 24%, 32%).
		•	Charitable & DAF Strategy: Configure specific years and tranches to transfer highly appreciated Taxable assets to a Donor Advised Fund, bypassing Capital Gains taxes and simulating charitable deductions.
	•	The Simulation (Runway Logic):
		•	Calculates RMDs based on dynamic start age.
		•	Applies progressive tax brackets to RMDs, Withdrawals, and Rental Yield.
		•	Embedded Gains Logic: Withdrawals from the Taxable bucket trigger proportionate Long-Term Capital Gains tax, preventing "free" liquidations of massive assets like CCRC buy-ins.
	•	Real Estate Lifecycle Engine:
		•	Models future property purchases by deducting capital from liquid assets in the target year.
		•	Applies annual maintenance drag to cashflow during ownership years.
		•	Models property liquidation by converting appreciated real estate equity back into liquid taxable assets in the sale year.
	•	Discretionary Budget:
		•	Manual entries feature double-click editing, toggle state persistence, and hard-delete confirmation.
