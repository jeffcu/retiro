This document outlines the acceptance criteria for the Curie Trust Financial Control Center.

## 2. Phase 0-9: Core Functionality (Completed)
All previous phases are stable.

---

## 3. Phase 10: The Strategic Tax Engine (Next)

**Objective**: Transform the forecast from a simple growth model to a tax-aware optimization engine.

### 10.1 Progressive Tax Logic
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Bracket Calculation**: Verify that $100k income results in a lower effective rate than $300k income (progressive vs flat).
- [ ] **Standard Deduction**: Verify that the first ~$29k (Joint) or ~$14k (Single) of income is tax-free.

### 10.2 RMDs (Required Minimum Distributions)
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Age Trigger**: Verify RMDs begin automatically at age 75 (or configured age).
- [ ] **Forced Withdrawal**: Verify that even if spending needs are met by Cash/Yield, the RMD is still withdrawn from the Deferred bucket and added to Taxable Income.

### 10.3 Roth Conversions (Bracket Filling)
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Filling the Bucket**: If the user sets a "Top of 22% Bracket" target, verify the system converts just enough Deferred assets to hit that limit, but no more.
- [ ] **Tax Impact**: Verify that taxes on the conversion are paid in the current year (ideally from Taxable brokerage).

---

## 4. Phase 11: Legacy & Liquidation (Future)

### 11.1 Liquidation Value
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Embedded Tax**: Verify the system displays a "Liquidation Value" that subtracts estimated taxes from the Deferred bucket (Ordinary Income) and Taxable bucket (Capital Gains).
- [ ] **Roth Premium**: Verify that $1 in Roth contributes exactly $1 to Liquidation Value, while $1 in Deferred contributes less (e.g., $0.76).
