## 3. Phase 10: The Strategic Tax Engine

### 10.1 - 10.5 Progressive Tax & Logic (Completed)
Stable.

### 10.6 Advanced Capital Evasion (DAFs)
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Tranche Execution**: Verify that scheduling a $100k DAF transfer in 2026 accurately removes $100k from the Taxable bucket in that year.
- [ ] **LTCG Bypass**: Verify that this transfer does *not* trigger the embedded LTCG tax penalty that a standard withdrawal would.

### 10.7 Real Estate Lifecycle Engine (Vacation Home Simulation)
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Future Purchase**: Add a property with a purchase year of Current Year + 5. Verify that in Year 5, the Liquid Assets drop by the exact purchase price, and Real Estate Equity increases accordingly.
- [ ] **Maintenance Drag**: Verify that during the years the property is owned, the total expenses line increases by the `annual_maintenance` amount (adjusted for inflation).
- [ ] **Liquidation Recoup**: Set a sale year for the property. Verify that in the sale year, the Real Estate Equity drops, and the compounded, appreciated value of the property is injected back into the Taxable Liquid Assets bucket.

## 4. Phase 11: Legacy & Liquidation (Future)

### 11.1 Liquidation Value
**Acceptance Criteria**:
- [ ] **Embedded Tax**: Verify the system displays a "Liquidation Value" that subtracts estimated taxes from the Deferred bucket (Ordinary Income) and Taxable bucket (Capital Gains).
- [ ] **Roth Premium**: Verify that $1 in Roth contributes exactly $1 to Liquidation Value, while $1 in Deferred contributes less (e.g., $0.76).
